#  Starbucks India Sales & Revenue Dashboard

An interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** built to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer ratings, and product category contributions across Starbucks stores in India.

> **Disclaimer:** This project uses an **AI-generated dataset** created for educational and portfolio purposes. The data is not affiliated with or representative of Starbucks' actual business operations.

##  Key Features
- KPI Tracking (Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value, Ratings)
- Revenue by Store Type Analysis
- Top Cities by Revenue
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Product Category Mix Analysis
- Interactive Slicers (State, City, Store Type, Month)

##  Key Insights
- Generated total revenue of **₹69.61 Cr** from **27.13 lakh orders**.
- Maintained an average order value of **₹276**.
- Achieved a strong customer rating of **4.4/5**.
- **Airport stores** contributed the highest revenue among all store types.
- **Delhi and Chennai** emerged as top-performing cities by revenue.
- Revenue peaked during **April–June**, indicating stronger seasonal demand.
- **Coffee products** accounted for the largest share of revenue (**44%**).
- Cold beverages contributed **20%** of total sales, making them the second-largest category.
- Bakery and snack items together contributed over **25%** of category revenue.
